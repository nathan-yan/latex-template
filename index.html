P1
\(y' + y = y^2, p(x) = 1, f(x) = 1, r = 2\)

Assume right-hand side is 0.
\(y' + y = 0 \\\Rightarrow \frac{y'}{y} = -1 \\\Rightarrow \ln{|y|} = -x + c\\y = ce^{-x}\)
Find a \(u\) that satisfies \(u'y(x) = f(x)(y(x))^ru^r\)
\(\Rightarrow u' \cdot e^{-x} = e^{-2x} \cdot u^2 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{u'}{u^2} = e^{-x} \\ \Rightarrow \int{\frac{u'}{u^2} du} = -e^{-x} + c \\ \Rightarrow -\frac{1}{u} = -e^{-x} + c \\ \Rightarrow u = \frac{1}{e^{-x} + c}\)
\(y = \frac{1}{e^{-x} + c} \cdot e^{-x} = \frac{1}{e^{-x}(1 + ce^{x})} \cdot e^{-x} = \boxed{\frac{1}{1 + ce^{x}}} \)


P3
\(x^2y' + 2y = 2e^{1/x}y^{1/2} \\ \Rightarrow y' + \frac{2}{x^2}y = \frac{2e^{1/x}}{x^2}y^{1/2}, \ p(x) = \frac{2}{x^2}, f(x) = \frac{2e^{1/x}}{x^2}, r = \frac{1}{2}\)
Assume right-hand side is 0.
\(\frac{y'}{y} = -\frac{2}{x^2} \\ \Rightarrow \ln{|y|} = \frac{2}{x} \\ \Rightarrow y = ue^{2 \cdot x^{-1}}\)
Find a \(u\) that satisfies \(u'y(x) = f(x)(y(x))^ru^r\)
\(\Rightarrow u' \cdot e^{2 \cdot x^{-1}} = \frac{2e^{1/x}}{x^2} \cdot e^{x^{-1}} \cdot \sqrt{u} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{u'}{u^{1/2}} = \frac{2}{x^2} \\ \Rightarrow \int{\frac{u'}{u^{1/2}} du} = -2x^{-1} + c \\ \Rightarrow 2u^{1/2} = -2x^{-1} + c \\ \Rightarrow u = -x^{-2} + c\)
\(y = \boxed{e^{2x^{-1}}(-x^{-2} + c)}\)


P7
\(y' - 2y = xy^3, \ p(x) = -2, f(x) = x, r = 3 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{y'}{y} = 2 \\ \Rightarrow \ln{|y|} = 2x \\ \Rightarrow y = e^{2x} \\\)
\(u' e^{2x} = x e^{6x}u^3 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{u'}{u^3} = xe^{4x} \\ \Rightarrow -\frac{1}{2}u^{-2} = \frac{1}{16}e^{4x}(4x - 1) \\ \Rightarrow u = \sqrt{-8\frac{1}{e^{4x}(4x - 1)}} \\ \Rightarrow u = 2\sqrt{2}e^{-2x}\frac{1}{\sqrt{4x - 1}}\)
\(y = \boxed{\frac{2\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{4x - 1}}}\)


P11
\(y' - 4y = \frac{48}{y^2}, y(0) = 1, p(x) = -4, f(x) = 48, r = -2 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{y'}{y} = 4 \\ \Rightarrow y = e^{4x}\\\)
\(u'e^{4x} = 48e^{-8x}u^{-2} \\ \Rightarrow u'u^2 = 48e^{-12x} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{1}{3}u^3 = -4e^{-12x} + c \\ \Rightarrow u = [-12e^{-12x} + c]^{1/3}\)
\(y = e^{4x} \cdot [-12e^{-12x} + c]^{1/3}, y(0) = 1 \\ \Rightarrow 1 = 1[-12 + c]^{1/3} \\ \Rightarrow c = 13 \\ \Rightarrow y = \boxed{e^{4x} \cdot [-12e^{-12x} + 13]^{1/3}}\)


P15
\(y' = \frac{y + x}{x} \\ \Rightarrow y' = \frac{y}{x} + 1 \\ \Rightarrow y' - \frac{y}{x} = 1, \ p(x) = -\frac{1}{x}, f(x) = 1, r = 0 \\ \Rightarrow \ln{|y|} = \ln{|x|} \\ \Rightarrow y = ux\)
\(u'x = x^0u^0 \\ \Rightarrow u'x = 1 \\ \Rightarrow u' = 1/x \\ \Rightarrow u = \ln{|x|} + c\)
\(y = \boxed{(\ln{|x|} + c)x}\)


P23
\(y' = \frac{xy + y^2}{x^2}, y(-1) = 2, \\ \Rightarrow y' = \frac{y}{x} + \frac{y^2}{x^2} \\ \Rightarrow y' - \frac{y}{x} = \frac{y^2}{x^2}, \ p(x) = -\frac{1}{x}, f(x) = \frac{1}{x^2}, r = 2\\ \Rightarrow \ln{|y|} = \ln{|x|} \\ \Rightarrow y = ux\)
\(u'x = \frac{1}{x^2}x^2u^2 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{u'}{u^2} = \frac{1}{x} \\ \Rightarrow -u^{-1} = \ln{|x|} + c \\ \Rightarrow u = -\frac{1}{\ln{|x|} + c} \)
\(y = -\frac{x}{\ln{|x|} + c}, y(-1) = 2 \\ \Rightarrow c = \frac{1}{2} \\ \Rightarrow y = \boxed{\frac{x}{\ln{|x|} + 0.5}}\)
